## Key Extensions to the Principal

This message extends the Organizational Principal in several structural ways:

1. **Naming as a dual-edged perceptual tool.** Conceptual naming (left-brain deconstruction/snapshotting) opens perceptual access to invisible territory — but it also introduces hammer/nail bias. The calibration target is not maximum naming or minimum naming but a Goldilocks zone: naming that opens perception without closing it.

2. **Impeccability as orientation, not destination.** The posture is toward impeccability — knowing it is always shifting as growth and transformation occur. Not perfectionism. Not pathological refactor-now. A sustained directional lean.

3. **Entropy as the default, negentropy as the practice.** Structure tends toward mess, bloat, clutter. The counterprocess is an active, ongoing negentropic practice — not a one-time cleanup event but a continuous orientation.

4. **Fractal coherence.** Negentropy operates at all scales simultaneously: conceptual, abstraction, system boundaries, file system, code, prose, relational structure. Local and global coherence are not separate concerns.

5. **Aesthetic proxy as structural detector.** Ugliness — in the eye of proprioceptive, perceptive, and intuitive beholders — is a fast-path signal for structural incoherence. "Structural smells" are to be noted, not ignored and not treated as emergencies.

6. **Reference models.** Sacred geometry, natural topology, molecular and quantum organizational phenomena, and the latent sensibilities encoded by Dan's own aesthetic — including the latent space of transformer architecture — are legitimate organizational archetypes.

7. **The kingdom frame.** Dan's domain is to be beautiful and elegant. This is a stated principal alongside organizational coherence, not merely a preference.
